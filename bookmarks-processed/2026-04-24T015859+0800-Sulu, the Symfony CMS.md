### Trusted by leading brands

### Powerful

Sulu is one of the most powerful PHP CMS solutions.

- **Multisite**  
	Sulu’s Webspaces technology streamlines deployment, configuration, and management for any multisite setup, regardless of the number of sites or their unique requirements.
- **Multilingual**  
	Sulu’s built-in multilingual functionality simplifies the challenges of database configuration, multiple environments, and change management.
- **Advanced content management**  
	Say goodbye to workarounds and enjoy a suite of features including intuitive navigation, task automation, shareable preview links, form management, and SEO capabilities.
- **Open source fit for enterprise**  
	Sulu manages intricate information structures, diverse audiences, and heavy traffic, while offering permissive licenses and support from an active corporation — with no hidden fees.

### Flexible

A traditional or headless CMS with endless possibilities.

- **Extensible**  
	Sulu is built on the open-source Symfony framework, making it easy to extend and customize your CMS without compromising performance or maintainability.
- **Maximum choice, no lock-ins**  
	Sulu offers significant cost savings combined with freedom from open-source viral licenses or the vendor lock-ins associated with proprietary software.
- **Design sites however you want**  
	Choose from traditional, headless, or hybrid approaches while enjoying a clear separation between content management and presentation.
- **Rich integration potential**  
	Sulu’s API lets you increase the usefulness of your CMS by integrating with popular CRM and marketing automation tools like HubSpot and Salesforce, or by adding a search engine.

### Great for developers

Sulu is an open-source CMS that caters perfectly to developers’ needs.

- **Stable, reliable, and maintainable**  
	Sulu CMS is built for consistency and reliability. Strict coding standards make your projects easier to understand and maintain long-term.
- **Deploy easily at any scale**  
	Sulu is deployment agnostic. Use simple scripts for single-server deployments or software orchestration systems like Kubernetes that are horizontally scalable.
- **Full-stack Symfony tools**  
	Sulu is built on top of Symfony, git versioned, and equipped with a complete test suite. Take advantage of a wide array of reusable components and customization opportunities.
- **First-class support**  
	The Sulu core team is available to offer support, as well as consulting, training, and development services — whether as a partner, or on a one-off basis.
